Track M11. CRPS + Diebold-Mariano on the headline panel.
==============================================================================================================

Setup    : SPY daily log excess growth; IS n=2516, OoS n=572; seed=20260422.
Ensemble : N_paths=1000 unconditional simulated paths per generator (cached in track_a).
CRPS     : sample CRPS via the unbiased sorted-ensemble identity.
DM       : Newey-West HAC variance, Bartlett kernel, bandwidth h = floor(T^(1/3)).

Reading  : Lower CRPS is better. DM > 0 means the reference (CHMM) loss exceeds the
           benchmark loss, i.e. CHMM is worse on CRPS by that test. p-values are two-sided.
