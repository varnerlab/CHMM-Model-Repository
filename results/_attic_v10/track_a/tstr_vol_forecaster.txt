==============================================================================================================
A4. TSTR (Train on Synthetic, Test on Real): HAR(1,5,22) vol forecaster
==============================================================================================================

Setup    : Target = daily squared return on SPY OoS window (572 obs).
Features : (intercept, lag-1 RV, mean RV over lags 1-5, mean RV over lags 1-22) (Corsi 2009).
Training : 50 synthetic paths per model, concatenated into one long series for OLS.
Metrics  : RMSE on y vs ŷ; QLIKE = mean(y/ŷ − log(y/ŷ) − 1). Lower is better on both.
Benchmark: HAR trained on real R_is (2014-2024) and evaluated on real R_oos (2024-2026).
