==============================================================================================================
Regime-conditional VaR back-test under one-step-ahead state forecast propagation
(peer-review B6)
==============================================================================================================
Setup: CHMM-N at K = 3 and K = 18 fit on SPY IS (T = 2516 days, seed = 20260420).
OoS  : T = 572 days. Parameters frozen at IS estimate; only the latent state
       belief updates as each new R_t arrives. At each OoS day t, conditional VaR
       VaR_α(t) = F^{-1}(α) where F is the K-component Gaussian mixture predictive
       density at t given F_{t-1} = IS ∪ R_oos[1..t-1] under the forward filter.
Tests: Kupiec LR_uc (unconditional coverage, ~χ²_1, critical 3.841)
       Christoffersen LR_ind (breach independence, ~χ²_1, critical 3.841)
       Christoffersen LR_cc = LR_uc + LR_ind (joint, ~χ²_2, critical 5.991)

Comparison panel: unconditional pooled-archive VaR for the same windows is in
results/diagnostics/var_christoffersen.txt and paper Appendix tab:christoffersen_var.
Both unconditional CHMM-N and GARCH(1,1) reject Christoffersen-ind on OoS at α=0.05;
the question this panel answers is whether regime forecast propagation closes that gap.
